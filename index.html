WebSTAC to Calendar

WUSTL schedule parser

Designed for students of Washington University in St. Louis.

Effortlessly put both classes and finals from WebSTAC into your Google Calendar.
  • No registration required.
  • Just copy and paste.
  • Schedule sent only to Google Calendar, and nobody else (privacy policy).
Scroll down to start OR About this site

② COPYPASTE

2a. Once you've logged into WebSTAC, select Courses & Registration >> Class Schedule.

2b. Easy way: select all (CTRL+A), and copy. Close this dialogue and paste into the text box.

Important: make sure you copied your schedule in LIST view, not grid view.
